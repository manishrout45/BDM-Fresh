Know Our Process

How We Produce Foods At Our Organic Food Processing Mills

Purity. Hygiene. Fresh - At Shree BDM, we believe that the right processing yields nutritious outcomes. Here’s a glimpse of how we produce food in our organic mills in Bhubaneswar, Odisha

Cleaning and Conditioning

Removing impurities and balancing moisture for milling.

Gristing

Grinding cleaned grains into smaller pieces, a prep-step before milling.

Milling

Blending grains for desired texture while retaining nutrients.

Preservation

Packed fresh and stored to preserve purity.
